We present a network-based, cross-species deep-learning model that predicts virus–host protein–protein interactions. We compared SARS-CoV-2 against other published coronaviruses through multiple sequence alignment of domains, functions, and mutations; built sequence-similarity and PPI graphs; and used graph embedding to train a cross-species model that predicts high-likelihood PPIs and infection pathways of SARS-CoV-2 across animal hosts.
