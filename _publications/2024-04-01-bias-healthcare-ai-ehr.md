We conducted a PRISMA-guided systematic review of bias detection and mitigation in EHR-based healthcare AI. Scripted keyword search yielded 200+ papers, which we manually screened and coded for evaluation metrics, detailed methods, and the types of bias addressed. The review summarizes the current landscape of bias-handling strategies in clinical AI and highlights gaps for future research.
