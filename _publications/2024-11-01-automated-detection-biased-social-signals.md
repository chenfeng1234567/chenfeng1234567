We combined ASR (Whisper) and speaker diarization (Pyannote) with multiple NLP and LLM models to detect biased social signals in patient–provider clinical conversations. We trained classifiers for patient- and clinician-side social-signal scores, examined how implicit racial bias surfaces in real clinical dialog, and benchmarked predictive performance across model families.
