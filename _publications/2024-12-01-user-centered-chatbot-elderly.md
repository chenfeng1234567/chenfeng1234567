We designed and deployed an LLM-powered chatbot in a retirement community to improve technology and eHealth literacy among older adults. The system was iterated through user interviews and training sessions, with accessibility-focused features such as adjustable font sizes, high-contrast modes, and voice-to-text. Prompts and the interface were engineered to maximize clarity and user satisfaction.
