We compared transformer-based and embedding-based NLP models (Mental-RoBERTa, SentenceBERT, LLaMA) and prompt-based LLM classification with DSM-5-informed prompts for PTSD detection from clinical interview transcripts. Error analysis on symptom severity and depression comorbidity reveals where models capture — and miss — clinically meaningful subtleties.
