This paper describes how we iteratively co-designed an LLM-powered chatbot with residents of a retirement community. Through interviews and usability sessions, we refined accessibility features — adjustable font sizes, high-contrast modes, voice-to-text — and engineered prompts for clear, age-appropriate responses. The work surfaces design principles for accessible LLM systems for older adults.
